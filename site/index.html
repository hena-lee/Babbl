macOS Menu Bar App

Don't type,
just speak.

Babbl is an offline voice-to-text app for macOS. Press a hotkey, speak, and your words appear wherever you're typing. No cloud. No subscription. Everything stays on your Mac.

Download for macOS View on GitHub

Free & open source · macOS 14+ · Apple Silicon

How it works

1

Press the hotkey

Double-tap Option (or set your own shortcut) to start recording from any app.

2

Speak naturally

A floating overlay shows your audio levels and a timer. Background media pauses automatically.

3

Text appears

Your speech is transcribed on-device, filler words are removed, and clean text is pasted into the active app.

Built for real use

On-Device Transcription

Powered by WhisperKit. Your audio never leaves your Mac — no internet connection required.

Smart Filler Removal

Automatically strips "um," "like," "you know," and other filler words with context-aware filtering. Keeps "I like dogs" intact.

Works Everywhere

Types directly into any app — Slack, VS Code, your browser, anything. Or copies to clipboard if you prefer.

Fast

Transcription completes in under a second on Apple Silicon. Multiple model sizes let you trade speed for accuracy.

Media Aware

Automatically pauses YouTube, Spotify, or any playing media when you start recording, then resumes when you're done.

Configurable

Choose your hotkey, pick a Whisper model, toggle individual filler words, and switch between typing and clipboard modes.

Your voice stays
on your Mac.

Babbl processes everything locally using Apple's Neural Engine. No audio is uploaded. No transcripts are sent anywhere. No analytics, no telemetry, no accounts. Your data is yours.

  • No internet connection needed
  • No cloud APIs or third-party services
  • No data collection of any kind
  • Transcription history is encrypted on disk
  • Open source — verify it yourself
